My hobbies outside of work include the following:

Reading

I love reading. I read 72 books last year and I hope to read as many (or more) this year. While I love most genres, my favorites are contemporary fiction, fantasy, sci-fi, and romance. Please don't ask me to pick a favorite book, it's impossible.

Crochet

I started crocheting in 2023 and it has quickly become another favorite hobby. I haven't started writing my own patterns yet, but my goal is to write my own pattern this year. I'm currently working on a laptop sleeve and a book blanket!

Watching TV & Movies

If I'm not reading or crocheting in my free time, I'm watching tv or a movie. I'm a big Star Wars fan, so I love a good sci-fi, but my comfort show is New Girl because it can always make me laugh. I enjoy almost everything: comedy, action, sci-fi, drama, and more.
